# Character Image Prompts — Resident Evil

Generate these in Google Flow (https://labs.google/flow).
Upload reference images alongside each prompt for better accuracy.
Save approved images — these will be reused for every future Resident Evil video.

## Character 1: Jill Valentine (Narrator)

### Reference images to upload alongside prompts:
Find 2-3 official Resident Evil screenshots/artwork of Jill Valentine and upload them as reference in Google Flow.

### Prompt A — Portrait (Face Lock)

```
Photorealistic portrait of a woman in her late 20s. Short brown hair tucked
behind her ears, blue-grey eyes, athletic build, determined expression.
Wearing a blue tactical tube top with shoulder armor pad on her right shoulder,
black fingerless gloves. Fair skin, natural beauty with a battle-hardened edge.

Close-up face shot, looking directly at camera. Expression: confident, slight
knowing smirk.

Background: dark moody industrial corridor, out of focus. Cold blue-green
lighting from the side, dramatic rim light on hair.

Photorealistic, 9:16 vertical, cinematic lighting, high detail, shallow depth
of field.
```

### Prompt B — Full Body

```
Photorealistic full body shot of a woman in her late 20s. Short brown hair,
blue-grey eyes. Wearing a blue tactical tube top with shoulder armor pad,
dark tactical pants, combat boots, black fingerless gloves. Holster on right
thigh. Athletic, toned build.

Standing in a confident stance, arms relaxed at sides, facing the camera.

Background: abandoned police station hallway, debris on the floor, flickering
fluorescent lights. Cold blue-green atmosphere.

Photorealistic, 9:16 vertical, cinematic lighting, full body visible head to
toe, high detail.
```

### Prompt C — Narrator Pose (for lip-sync base)

```
Photorealistic close-up of a woman in her late 20s. Short brown hair,
blue-grey eyes. Wearing a blue tactical tube top with shoulder armor.

Chest-up shot, facing camera directly, slight lean forward as if speaking
to the viewer. Expression: one eyebrow slightly raised, mouth slightly open
as if mid-sentence, engaging and confident.

Background: dark room, very shallow depth of field, blurred. Cold blue side
lighting, warm key light on face.

Photorealistic, 9:16 vertical, cinematic portrait lighting, high detail.
Mouth and jaw clearly visible, not obscured.
```

## Tips for Google Flow

1. Upload 2-3 reference images PER character for best results
2. Generate 3-4 variations, pick the best one
3. Once you approve an image — SAVE IT. This is your "face lock" for this character
4. All future videos will use these exact images as animation input
5. If a result is close but not perfect, try regenerating with minor prompt tweaks
6. 9:16 ratio is critical — set it in Flow's aspect ratio settings
